Every week, Steve Hargadon and I sit down (virtually) to talk about the latest ed-tech news. I always find our conversation to be one of the most thought-provoking exchanges I have all week.

We actually missed our weekly podcast last week, thanks to the demands of our travel/conferences schedules.  But we were back on track this week, covering 14+ days of ed-tech happenings.  There were also some technical problems with this week's recording -- ugh, Skype.  But Steve was able to correct the issue, demonstrating one might argue that podcasting software is not a MacGuffin.

Yes, we talk a lot about MacGuffins in this week's episode, among other things.  The link to the MP3 is below, and/or you can subscribe to the podcast feed here.  You can also find the podcast in iTunes, as part of Steve's EdTechLive series.
