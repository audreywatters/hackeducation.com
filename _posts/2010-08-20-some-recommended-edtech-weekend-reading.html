"E-learning benefits teachers, not just students," Silicon Republic: A study from Boston College has tracked the impact of online professional development for teachers, finding it improved both their teaching methods and their knowledge of the subject itself.

"Tracking Independent Reading in High School," The Thinking Stick: Jeff Utrecht details the tools his school used in order to create student reading portfolios, including Google Apps and Goodreads.

"Xplana.com: Is This a PLE?," e-Literate: Michael Feldman examines LMSs, the textbook industry, and edupunk, by way of introducing Xplana, an edtech startup that "lives at an interesting crossroads of these three potential disruptions."
