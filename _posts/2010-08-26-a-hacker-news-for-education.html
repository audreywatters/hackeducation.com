Hacker News is one of my favorite resources for finding good tech stories. It's run by Y Combinator, the premier startup incubator program, and the site allows anyone to submit tech-related stories - vote them up and down, offer comments. It may well be the site for sharing and crowdsourcing tech industry news and tech-oriented blogs.

I've long wished the edtech community had a better place to do just that: share news and links, aggregate and support all the great resources that are out there.

And now we do, thanks to the online gradebook LearnBoost, who've taken the Hacker News model and applied it to the education technology space.

Submit your stories. Vote and comment on the posts you like. And subscribe to the RSS feed, so that you can easily track what's trending in education technology.

(Thanks LearnBoost.)
