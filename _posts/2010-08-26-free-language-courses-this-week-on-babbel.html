Unless you've been fortunate enough to spend your summer holidays abroad, you might need to have your language skills refreshed before school starts.

With that in mind, online language learning company Babbel is offering its courses -- which include the online learning material, iPhone app and downloadable desktop app -- for new users to explore, free, until Sunday. For more information, visit http://www.babbel.com/go/one-free-week
