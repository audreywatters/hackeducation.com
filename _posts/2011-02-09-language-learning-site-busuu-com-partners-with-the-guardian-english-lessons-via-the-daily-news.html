Language-learning site busuu.com has announced a new partnership with The Guardian and Macmillan Education.

The partnership integrates busuu.com's language learning community with a daily newspaper, arguably one of the best ways to improve your real-world English reading skills. This feature will be available for premium users of busuu.com, who'll be able to practice and improve their English skills through the Guardian's news articles, with an integrated dictionary provided by Macmillan.

This announcement is the latest in a series of partnerships that language-learning sites have struck with other communications companies. Recently, Livemocha inked a deal to offer discounted English lessons to subscribers of Telefonica Brazil.
