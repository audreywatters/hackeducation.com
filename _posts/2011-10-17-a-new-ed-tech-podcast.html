I'm thrilled to announce that Steve Hargadon and I have started recording a podcast together, based on my weekly roundup posts and on the stories I've covered throughout the week here on Hack Education.

It's a huge honor to be working with Steve on this endeavor as I see him as one of the key people who's pioneered the adoption of social networking tools for teachers and students, who's argued for the importance of edu-blogging, and who's long supported open source technology in education.

We make a good podcasting team too, even though this first episode runs a little long (that's an indication of our enthusiasm for this new project, I think).
