Every week, Steve Hargadon and I sit down (virtually) to talk about the latest ed-tech news. I always find our conversation to be one of the most thought-provoking exchanges I have all week.

This week, we spent a lot of time talking about the surprising news from Blackboard, with its acquisition of Moodlerooms and its unveiling of an open source initiative. What are the implications on open source technologies, open source communities, learners' data? Well, listen in to hear our thoughts. We also talk about the Atlanta-Journal-Constitution's story about "irregularities" in testing data nationwide.

The link to the MP3 of the show is below, and/or you can subscribe to the podcast feed. You can also find the podcast in iTunes, as part of Steve's EdTechLive series.
