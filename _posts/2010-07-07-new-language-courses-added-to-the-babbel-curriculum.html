Online language learning company Babbel announced today that it was adding three new language courses to their curriculum: Beginning Swedish, Beginning Brazilian Portugese, and Advanced English.

Late last month, the Berlin-based company introduced a browser-based voice recognition tool to help learners with their pronunciation. The tool is integrated into all the company's language courses. Babbel also has an iPhone to facilitate language learning on-the-go.
