Every week, Steve Hargadon and I sit down (virtually) to talk about the latest ed-tech news. I always find our conversation to be one of the most thought-provoking exchanges I have all week.  This week, we were actually face-to-face for the recording as we were both in attendance at the DML conference in San Francisco.

Our conversation involved wikis, Late Nite Labs, Gates Foundation's investment patterns, my lack of enthusiasm for Seth Godin's writings, and more.
