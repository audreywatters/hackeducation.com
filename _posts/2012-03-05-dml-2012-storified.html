When I go to conferences these days, I find myself taking half my notes in Evernote and the other half on Twitter. That makes organizing my thoughts afterwards more than a little messy, particularly if I don't write blog posts right away. And thanks to the Twitter's craptastic search function, it's not always easy to go back in time to rediscover what others Tweeted at an event. So I decided to Storify my notes, my Tweets, others' blog posts and videos from DML 2012.

Warning: after 3 days of sessions, this page will probably take a while to load.  Also: I didn't include any of the great meal-time and drinks-time conversations I had -- because halfway through someone always turns to me and says "This is off the record, right?"  Right...
