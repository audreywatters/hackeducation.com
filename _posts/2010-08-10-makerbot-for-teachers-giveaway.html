File this under News that Makes Me Want Back in the Classroom:

MakerBot, the open source robot that helps people make robots that make things (which, you gotta admit, is pretty much the coolest thing ever), is giving away 10 of its Cupcake CNC kits to the 10 teachers who can best demonstrate their plans for incorporating the MakerBot into their course plans. Submissions will be judged on innovation, clarity and curriculum integration.

For complete details, see the MakerBot blog. Deadline for submissions is August 23.
